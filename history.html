The Development of Deepfake Technology

Deepfake technology has evolved rapidly, with its origins in early research in deep learning and neural networks. It began as a tool for enhancing image and video synthesis but soon found new applications in entertainment, social media, and even politics. Here's a look at key milestones and early applications:

Early Origins

The roots of deepfake technology trace back to the advancements in deep learning and neural networks. Early research focused on image generation and enhancement, laying the groundwork for more complex systems.

FaceSwap & DeepFaceLab

Tools like FaceSwap and DeepFaceLab revolutionized deepfake creation by providing accessible, open-source platforms for users to swap faces in videos. These tools played a key role in the popularization of deepfakes.

Early Applications

Deepfakes were initially used in entertainment, social media, and viral content creation. However, they soon found controversial uses in politics and news, raising significant ethical concerns.
