History of Deepfakes

Home History Detection Techniques

Deepfake technology origins in early AI and neural network research.

The first deepfake videos emerge in 2017, shocking the media.

FaceSwap: A tool that helped popularize deepfakes for public use.

DeepFaceLab: A widely used tool for creating highly realistic deepfakes.

Deepfakes begin impacting social media, with viral videos spreading misinformation.

Deepfakes gain political significance, raising concerns over fake news.

Celebrity deepfakes become increasingly popular and controversial.

Efforts to detect and counter deepfakes lead to the development of detection tools.
